Ingredients:
 - 3 cups pasta
 - 6 garlic gloves sliced
 - 1 spoon vegetable oil
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon ground pepper
 - 1 bunch of parsley
 - some cheese

Directions:
 1. Boil the pasta for six minutes and wash it after wards to stop the cooking
 process.

 2. Put oil in the pan and heat it then add the garlic.

 3. Add salt and pepper.

 4. Put the mixture on the pasta and stir them together.

 5. Add the cheese on the pasta.

 6. Use the parsley for decoration.
